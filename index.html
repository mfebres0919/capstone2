Why did I make the switch?

I chose to pursue web development because of its flexibility—it allows me to work from anywhere and adapt to different environments while building creative and impactful solutions.

I was drawn to web development because of the creative freedom it offers. It allows me to bring ideas to life, design unique user experiences, and solve problems in innovative ways, all while expressing my creativity through code.

Web development brings me a sense of joy and fufillment that I haven’t found in any other field. I love the process of building something from the ground up, solving challenges, and seeing my work come to life—it’s rewarding in a way that keeps me motivated and excited to keep learning.
